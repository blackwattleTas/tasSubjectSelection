Why choose a TAS subject at Blackwattle Bay Campus?

The modern world revolves around technology. Undertaking these subjects will empower you to be a creator with technology rather than just a consumer.

Here at Blackwattle Bay we have a TAS (Technology and Applied Studies) faculty with 7 highly qualified and passionate teachers and something facilities. Something Something Something.

As well as learning about various technologies and developing skills, choosing a TAS subject will help develop a range of 21st century skills including :

  • Project management
  • Collaboration
  • Effective communication
  • Problem solving
  • Creativity

If you have an interest in technology and a passion for tinkering and building things then a TAS subject at Blackwattle Bay Campus may be a subject you would find very valuable.

Our subjects are open to all students regardless of existing experience or level of ability. You do not need to have studied technologies based subjects in Stage 5 (Years 9 and 10) in order to access and succeed in any of these subjects.
